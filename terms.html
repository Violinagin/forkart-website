B. Collaborative Lineage Splits

ForkArt's collaborative pay system automatically distributes a portion of tips to artists whose work contributed to a piece through the remix chain. Split percentages are calculated based on the lineage structure at the time of the tip. All splits are visible to contributors. ForkArt is not responsible for disputes between contributors regarding split percentages.
