ForkArt is a social art remix platform that allows users to view, upload, and interact with creative works.

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using the app.

By using ForkArt, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Account Access & Viewing the App

No account is required to browse and view the public gallery.

An account is required to upload artwork or use social features such as commenting, liking, or remixing content.

Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

When you create an account or participate in social features, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Artworks or other creative content you upload

Currently, the app supports image uploads. In the future, the app may support additional creative formats such as audio, video, or writing.

B. User-Generated Content

Any content you upload (including images and future supported formats) may be visible to other users depending on how the app is designed at the time of posting.

You are responsible for the content you choose to share.

C. Usage & Technical Data

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • App interactions (screens viewed, features used)
  • Crash reports and error logs

This data helps us improve stability, performance, and user experience.

Analytics

ForkArt uses Google Analytics to understand how users interact with the app. This information is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form and may include:

  • App usage patterns
  • General device information

Google Analytics operates under its own privacy policy.

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app may store limited information locally, including:

  • Login tokens to keep you signed in
  • App preferences and settings
  • Temporarily cached images to improve performance

You can clear this data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

Data Stored on Our Servers

We may store:

  • Account information (email, username)
  • Artworks and other content you upload
  • Social interactions (comments, likes)
  • Technical logs used for debugging and maintenance

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Deletion & Retention

You may delete your account at any time through the app settings.

When an account is deleted:

  • Account information and associated content are removed from active use
  • Content may be temporarily retained for a limited period to allow for recovery, dispute resolution, or legal compliance

Any retained data is securely stored and permanently deleted within a reasonable timeframe, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

You may request confirmation of deletion or ask questions about data retention by contacting us at forkartapp@gmail.com

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Enable social and creative features
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Respond to support requests
  • Maintain safety and prevent misuse

Payments & Monetization (Future Feature)

ForkArt does not currently process payments.

In the future, the app may allow artists to receive payments. If this feature is added, this Privacy Policy will be updated to clearly explain how payment-related data is handled.
