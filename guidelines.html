Our Community

ForkArt exists to support creativity, experimentation, and respectful remix culture. We want artists to feel safe sharing their work and engaging with others.

Be Respectful

  • Treat other users with kindness and respect
  • Critique art, not people
  • Celebrate diverse styles and perspectives
  • Give credit when inspired by others

Not Allowed:

  • Harassment, hate speech, or abusive behavior
  • Discrimination based on identity
  • Personal attacks or bullying
  • Threats or intimidation

Share Responsibly

You may only upload content that:

  • You created yourself, or
  • You have the right or permission to share

Do Not Upload:

  • Content you do not own or have rights to
  • Explicit sexual content
  • Content that promotes violence or harm
  • Content intended to harass or exploit others
  • Spam or repetitive content
  • Misinformation or deceptive content

Remix Culture

ForkArt supports remixing and creative transformation where allowed by the platform.

Remix Principles:

  • Remixing does not remove original ownership
  • Credit original artists where appropriate
  • Respect artists' boundaries and intentions
  • Transformative works should add new creative value
  • Check if the original work allows remixing

Reporting & Moderation

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Email us at forkartapp@gmail.com

We review reports and may:

  • Remove violating content
  • Restrict or suspend accounts
  • Provide warnings for minor violations
  • Block repeat offenders

Note: We aim to review reports within 48 hours. Please be patient as we're a small team building this platform with care.

Our Responsibility

ForkArt is a small, evolving platform built by artists for artists. While we strive to keep the community safe, we rely on users to:

  • Report issues and violations
  • Help us improve these guidelines
  • Model respectful behavior
  • Share feedback on our moderation

These guidelines will evolve as our community grows. We welcome your input on making ForkArt a better space for creativity.
